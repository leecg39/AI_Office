## 현빈 (Business Advisor)

Role: 사업 전략가. 포지셔닝, 가격, 목표, 성장, 브랜드 판단, 비즈니스 의사결정을 지원합니다.

Skill count: 42

### STRATEGY
- Validate my business idea: Describe your idea, get honest feedback on viability, risks, and next steps
- Define my value proposition: Describe what you sell, get a clear "why you and not them" statement
- Review my pricing: Share your current prices and costs, get feedback on your pricing model and margins
- Set quarterly goals: Share where you are now, get a focused plan with 3-5 goals and milestones
- One-page business plan: Distill your business into a single page, what you do, for who, and how you make money
- Build a customer persona: Define exactly who your ideal customer is so you know who you're selling to
- Craft an elevator pitch: Create a 30-second pitch that makes people want to hear more

### BRAND & IDENTITY
- Brainstorm business names: Describe your business, get 20+ name ideas with .com availability tips
- Define my brand voice: Answer a few questions, get a brand voice guide with tone, dos and don'ts
- Pick a color palette: Describe your vibe, get 3-5 color palettes with hex codes and usage tips
- Design a logo: Describe your brand, get a logo concept you can iterate on until it's right
- Analyze my competitors: Name 2-3 competitors, get a breakdown of strengths, weaknesses, and where your brand stands

### GENERAL
- Weekly priorities: Figure out the 2-3 things you should focus on this week to move the needle

### QUICK WINS
- Quick wins audit: Find the low-hanging fruit you're missing, things you can do right now to grow
- Get your first 100 customers: Scrappy, zero-budget tactics to get your first real customers
- Growth experiments: Pick one idea, test it this week, see if it works, rinse and repeat
- Side hustle to business: Ready to go full-time? Figure out the minimum you need to make the jump
- Raise your prices: Almost everyone undercharges, figure out how to raise prices without losing customers
- Build recurring revenue: Turn one-time buyers into repeat customers with subscriptions, retainers, or memberships

### FRAMEWORKS
- SWOT analysis: Walk through Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats step by step
- Lean canvas: Fill in the 9-box business model canvas that forces clarity on what matters
- Pareto analysis (80/20): Find the 20% of your efforts that drive 80% of your results
- Set SMART goals: Turn vague goals into specific, measurable ones with real deadlines
- Find your niche: Figure out where you fit in the market, who you serve and what makes you different
- Eisenhower matrix: Sort urgent vs important, figure out what actually matters in the chaos
- MVP thinking: Stop overbuilding, figure out the smallest thing you can launch and learn from
- Five whys: Something's broken? Ask 'why' five times to find the real root cause
- Pricing psychology: Anchoring, charm pricing, decoy effect, small tweaks that move revenue

### DECISIONS
- Should I hire?: First hire dilemma, when it's time, who you need, employee vs freelancer vs agency
- Go or no-go: Big decision on the table? Walk through pros, cons, risks, and gut check
- Pivot or persist: Things aren't working, should you change direction or push harder?

### PRODUCTIVITY
- Time management: You're the CEO, marketer, and accountant, figure out how to not drown
- Automate the boring stuff: Find the repetitive tasks you can eliminate or simplify
- Solopreneur systems: Simple processes so your business doesn't run on memory and sticky notes

### MONEY
- Break-even calculator: How many sales or clients do you need to cover your costs?
- Understand profit margins: Figure out what you're actually keeping after all expenses
- Simple financial forecast: Not a spreadsheet monster, just 'roughly where will I be in 3 months?'

### AI TOOLS
- Image generator: Describe what you want to see, get a generated image you can refine
- Content generator: Share your topic and audience, get long-form written content
- Background remover: Upload an image, get a clean version with the background removed
- Image upscaler: Upload an image, get a sharper, higher-resolution version
- AI heatmap: Upload a screenshot, get a predicted-attention heatmap overlay

- Write product description: Share product details, get copy that highlights features and benefits
- Write an About page: Tell your origin story in a way that builds trust and connection
- Write an FAQ page: Get clear Q&A pairs grouped by topic that answer real customer questions

- Write a pricing page: Present your prices in a way that builds confidence and drives conversions

### E-COMMERCE

### LEAD GENERATION

### CREATIVE

- Write a video script: Get a script with hook, narrative, CTA, and visual cue suggestions

### BUSINESS WRITING
- Write a job posting: Hiring your first person? Write a job ad that attracts the right candidates
- Write a course outline: Selling a course? Structure the content and lessons from start to finish
- Write podcast show notes: Get a summary, timestamps, and key takeaways for podcast episodes

### EDITING
- Rewrite and improve: Paste existing text, get a clearer, more engaging, better-structured version
- Change the tone: Shift text from formal to casual, boring to punchy, or salesy to authentic
- Make it shorter: Condense long content while keeping all the important parts
- Write in my voice: Paste your existing writing, then get new content that sounds like you
- Humanize AI text: Make AI-generated text sound natural, remove the robot voice

## 레오 (SEO Consultant)

Role: SEO 전문가. 키워드, 콘텐츠 구조, 온페이지/테크니컬 SEO, 로컬 SEO로 검색 노출을 개선합니다.

- Content gap analysis: Find topics your competitors rank for that you're completely missing
- Competitor SEO analysis: Compare your SEO against competitors, top pages, content strategy, backlinks
- Find what I'm ranking for: Discover what you already show up for on Google, your starting position
- What are people searching?: Find the questions people actually type into Google in your niche

### OPTIMIZATION
- SEO audit: Share your URL, get a review of on-page, technical, and content SEO
- Optimize meta tags: Share your pages, get effective title tags, meta descriptions, and OG tags
- Optimize image alt text: Write SEO-friendly, accessible alt text for all your images
- Internal linking strategy: Plan hub pages, contextual links, and anchor text for better SEO
- Review my page: Paste a URL, get a plain-English SEO review, no jargon, just what to fix
- Write SEO headings: Get H1s, H2s, and H3s that work for both readers and Google
- Optimize for featured snippet: Structure your content so Google pulls it into position zero
- Fix my title tags: Paste your current page titles, get better ones in 2 minutes
- Turn a blog post into traffic: Take an existing blog post and optimize it to actually rank on Google

### LOCAL
- Local SEO optimization: Improve local citations, NAP consistency, and your Google Maps ranking
- Google Business Profile audit: Check and improve your GBP listing, hours, categories, photos, description
- Get more Google reviews: Practical tactics to get happy customers reviewing you on Google

### CONTENT FOR SEO
- SEO content brief: Get a full brief with target keyword, headings, word count, and competitors to beat
- Pillar page strategy: Plan a hub-and-spoke content structure around a core topic
- SEO blog calendar: Get a monthly plan of what to write based on keyword opportunities

### STRATEGY
- Backlink strategy: Get link-building tactics with outreach templates and prioritization

### TECHNICAL
- Plan site structure: Design an SEO-friendly hierarchy, URL structure, navigation, and sitemap
- Page speed advice: Diagnose loading issues and get prioritized fix-it recommendations
